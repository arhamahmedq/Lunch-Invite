A Message To The Brothers

To all the brothers,

I just wanted to say thank you for every moment we've shared — from random nonsense conversations, stupid jokes that somehow became legendary, times we laughed until we couldn't breathe, and all the memories that slowly became part of our lives.

Life has a weird way of moving everyone onto different roads. People get busy, schedules get packed, and before we realize it, the moments we thought would happen forever slowly become memories.

So this meetup isn't just another lunch.

This might genuinely be one of the last times all of us are together in the same place before everyone starts chasing different goals and different chapters of life.

Years later we probably won't remember assignments, stress, or random problems, but we'll remember who was sitting around the table laughing over food.

So if possible, come.
Not because attendance is compulsory.
Come because one day we might wish we had one more chance to sit together again.

The Rothschild Brothers deserve one final chapter.
